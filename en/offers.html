The B2B Sales Sprint.

Take your B2B sales team from a 20% close rate to 35%+ in eight weeks. Without hiring new reps. Without new tools. Without you running the engagement.

This is built for you if
  • +You have 2–10 sales reps, or you're personally the Head of Sales by default.
  • +Your B2B close rate sits in the 15–25% range and feels stuck there.
  • +You sell deals in the €10k–€300k band, with cycles of 2–12 weeks.
  • +Your team is good on product but freezes on negotiation, follow-up, and closing.
This is not built for you if
  • –You want me to run paid ads or "do your marketing".
  • –You're hoping for a generic webinar-style sales training.
  • –You're not personally willing to be in the weekly revenue meeting.
  • –You'd rather hire a fractional CRO who's never personally closed a real deal.

What you get over eight weeks

Deliverables
– 01

Full sales-process diagnostic

A line-by-line read of your last 50 closed and lost deals, plus shadowing 10 live calls. You'll see where you actually lose deals – usually not where you think.

– 02

Custom B2B sales playbook

Qualification, discovery, objection handling and follow-up cadence – written specifically for your product, your buyers and your sales motion. The team uses this, not a binder on a shelf.

– 03

Live coaching on 10+ real sales calls

In-the-moment debriefs after each call. No role-play, no whiteboard theatre – just feedback applied to deals already in your pipeline.

– 04

Weekly 1:1 coaching for every rep

Four weeks. Each rep gets one focused hour with me to work the deals on their own desk.

– 05

Pipeline-by-source close-rate analysis

A simple dashboard that finally tells you which lead source is paying you back and which is bleeding margin.

– 06

Weekly revenue meeting, facilitated

For four weeks I run your revenue meeting. Then you get the template – agenda, scorecard, rep questions – and you keep running it.

The Embedded Revenue Operator.

For B2B founders in the €1–10M range who are still the de-facto Head of Revenue: I take that role off your shoulders one day a week. You stop being the bottleneck. Revenue grows without you.

This is built for you if
  • +You're the founder/CEO and still personally close the biggest deals.
  • +You have a sales team but no senior sales leader above them.
  • +Hiring a full-time CRO is premature, but going another year founder-led is dangerous.
  • +You'd rather pay senior money for one day a week than junior money for five.
This is not built for you if
  • –You want a placeholder you can ignore for 5 months and review at month 6.
  • –You aren't ready to give an outside operator authority over the revenue meeting.
  • –You're under €1M revenue – the Sprint is the better starting point.
  • –You want a coach, not an operator. This is a seat, not advice.
