02What I do

Growth that doesn't depend on you. I work with Estonian B2B founders in the €1–10M band who are still the de-facto Head of Sales — and know that's the bottleneck.

— I

Diagnose the sales motion

A line-by-line read of your last fifty deals and a set of live calls, to find where you actually lose business — usually not where you think. You see the real bottleneck before we touch anything.

— II

Build the playbook, coach the team

A written sales playbook the team actually uses, weekly coaching of every rep on live deals, and — when it helps — me sitting in the chair to close one or two strategic deals myself.

— III

Install the operating rhythm

Hiring, onboarding, the weekly revenue meeting, pricing reviews — the cadence that lets revenue grow without you in every deal. An engine you can run after I've gone.

03How I work

No decks. No theatre. Inside the company, alongside the founder.

I carry a quota of my own. If the engagement isn't lifting revenue and leaving you with a team that can close without me, I'm not doing my job.

Most consultants leave you with a deck. I leave you with a team that closes more deals, a written playbook they actually use, and an operating rhythm you can run after I've gone.

Engagements are small, named, and personal. I take on no more than two to three companies at a time. I work in English and Estonian, and I don't pretend to be a firm I'm not.

If a conversation reveals I'm wrong for you, I'll tell you in the first call. That's a feature of the practice, not a bug.
